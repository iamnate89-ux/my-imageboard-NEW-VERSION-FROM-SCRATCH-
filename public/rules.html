Rules & Guidelines

Notice: By posting on this website, you agree to follow all rules listed below. Violations may result in a warning, post deletion, or a permanent ban at the discretion of staff.

Global Rules

  1. Rule 1. Do not post, request, or link to any content that is illegal in the United States.
  2. Rule 2. Do not post or solicit personal information about other users.
  3. Rule 3. Users who are under the age of 16 are forbidden from posting.
  4. Rule 4. Do not spam, flood, or deliberately disrupt threads.
  5. Rule 5. Do not post pornography. This is an safe for work website. Real gore is allowed to be posted on the board it is allowed in (ex: /b/, /pol/) only if it is in an academic, documenting context relevant to the topic without glorification.
  6. Rule 6. Do not impersonate staff or other users.
  7. Rule 7. Do not post malicious links, viruses, or anything intended to harm other users.
  8. Rule 8. Respect other users. Harassment, racism, and targeted hate / doxxing are not tolerated.
  9. Rule 9. Ban evasion is forbidden.
  10. Rule 10. Do not post copyrighted content.

Posting Guidelines

  1. Keep posts relevant to the board topic.
  2. Do not create duplicate threads — use the catalog to check if a thread already exists.
  3. Use greentext (>text) and reply quotes (>>postNo) appropriately.
  4. Do not bump (reply to) old threads without meaningful contribution.
  5. Posts that contain real gore imagery must be marked as mature. The mature tag does not allow users to post pornography. It is recommended that a user is 18+ to view images tagged as mature unblurred.

Bans & Appeals

  1. Bans are issued at staff discretion based on rule violations.
  2. Attempting to evade a ban by switching IPs will result in a permanent ban.
  3. If you believe your ban was issued in error, type your appeal in the appeal box of the ban screen.
  4. Permanent bans are forbidden from appealing for violations of Rule 1.
  5. Please be considerate to report rule breaking posts to staff. We cannot see every post that violates our guidelines, but your contribution will keep this site safe for everyone.
